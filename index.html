♠ Range Poker ♥

No Limit Hold'em
Standard heads-up No Limit Texas Hold'em with a 52-card deck. Assign actions to your full 13×13 starting hand range each street.
Limit Hold'em
Standard Limit Texas Hold'em. Fixed bet and raise sizes. Limit of 4 bets per street.
Short Deck Hold'em
36-card deck (6 and up). 9×9 range grid, 630 combos. Flushes beat Full Houses. Ace plays low in A-6-7-8-9 straights.
Hollywood
Each player gets one face-up card (visible to both) and two face-down cards. Full 13×13 range grid for the face down cards.
Half Hollywood minigame
Like Hollywood, but with only one face down card. Range chart is a 4×13 grid of single cards (one cell per possible face-down card).
Aces Cracked minigame
BB is always dealt A♠A♥ face-up and can only call or fold preflop. Practice playing SB against a known overpair or BB defending with one.
